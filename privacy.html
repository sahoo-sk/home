Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At Sk. Sahoo, accessible from this website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by us and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us. For example, we collect information when you fill out a contact form, subscribe to a newsletter, or communicate with us. The types of information we may collect include your:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message content

2. Log Files

We follow a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, Sk. Sahoo uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

4. Third Party Privacy Policies

Our Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information.

5. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
